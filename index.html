Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation drives Bangladesh 2.0 by advancing technology, entrepreneurship, and creativity, fostering global leadership and solutions.

Sustainability drives Bangladesh 2.0, prioritizing renewable energy, climate resilience, urban planning, and resource preservation.

Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure underpins Bangladesh 2.0, enabling growth through transport, digital connectivity, smart cities, and development.

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh 2.0 ensures inclusion through education, skill training, gender equity, and equal opportunities.
